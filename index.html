Bleak World

What is Bleak World?

Bleak World is a...

  • ...Dungeon World hack
  • ...grimdark setting
  • ...time after the Age of Heroes has long passed
  • ...time when greed and corruption thrive
  • ...world of flawed men and women

[DEBTS, MASTERS, FLAWS, MISFITS]

At the core of Bleak World is the idea of flaws. Flaws...

  • ...highlight character's frailties.
  • ...allow players to showoff their weakness
  • ...allow characters to succeed in spite of themselves.
  • ...create humor
  • ...create sympathy
  • ...and allow characters to redeem themselves.

The Moves

Core Moves

All Hell Breaks Loose

When you act despite an imminent threat or suffer a calamity, say how you deal with it and roll. If you do it by...

  • ...by powering through, +Body
  • ...using charm and social grace, +Face
  • ...by getting out the way or acting fast, +Hand
  • ...with cunning or quick thinking, +Mind
  • ...by exerting your will, +Spirit

On a 10+ you set out to do what you intended, the threat doesn't come to bear. On a 7-9, you stumble hesitate or flinch: the GM will offer you a worse outcome, hard bargain or ugly choice.

The Playbooks

The Sorcerer

Gone are the ancient magi who mastered the arcane arts. All that is left is you. Your magic is a pale shadow to their ancient masterworks. Yet you somehow eke out a modest living doing what others cannot (or won't) do. You've evaded the witch hunters, mastered enough of your art not to be a danger to yourself. Now the real trouble start.

1. Choose a name

Circe, Morgan, Prospero, Xeno, or Zeranthus

2. Choose a title

Magician, Sorcerer, Warlock, Witch, or Wizard

3. Stats

Your stats are Body -1, Face -1, Hand +0, Mind +1, Spirit +1

4. Choose a look

Choose one for each:

Haunted eyes, sharp eyes, crazy eyes

Skull cap, wild hair, shaved head

Black robes, weird robes, worn robes

Bent body, pudgy body, thin body

5. Choose a flaw

Choose a flaw and gain the corresponding move.

Wicked

When you succeed at an Aid, or Parlay move on a roll of 10+ you can choose to fail that roll. If you do hold 1 and choose one:

  • You whisper something to the character, making them Disturbed.
  • You scratch or bite the character, dealing 1d4 damage.

You may spend hold to succeed with a 10+ when you roll 9- when all hell breaks loose. Choose one of the following:

  • Hell isn't ready for you, dark forces intervene to allow you to succeed.
