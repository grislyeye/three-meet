Bleak World

Introduction

Bleak World is a grimdark Dungeon World hack. It is a world where the age of heroes is long past. A world where greed and corruption thrive. A world of flawed men and women.

Play it if you want to:

  • Perform dark deeds in a grim world
  • Play anti-heroes who only succeed in spite of themselves

The Sorcerer

Gone are the ancient magi who mastered the arcane arts. All that is left is you. Your magic is a pale shadow to their ancient masterworks. Yet you somehow eke out a modest living doing what others cannot (or won't) do. You've evaded the witch hunters, mastered enough of your art not to be a danger to yourself. Now the real trouble start.

1. Choose a name

2. Choose a title

Magician, Sorcerer, Warlock, Witch, or Wizard

3. Stats

Your stats are Body -1, Face -1, Hand +0, Mind +1, Spirit +1

4. Choose a look

Choose on for each:

Haunted eyes, sharp eyes, crazy eyes

Skull cap, wild hair, shaved head

Black robes, weird robes, worn robes

Bent body, pudgy body, thin body

5. Choose a flaw

Choose a flaw and gain the corresponding move.

Wicked

When you succeed at an Aid or Hinder, or Parlay move on a roll of 10+ you can choose to fail that roll. If you do hold 1 and choose one:

  • You whisper a terrible secret to the character making them Disturbed.
  • You scratch or bite the character, making them hostile to you or weakening your bond.

You may spend hold to accidentally succeed at a failed move.
