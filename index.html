Bleak World

Introduction

Bleak World is a grimdark Dungeon World hack. It is a world where the age of heroes is long past. A world where greed and corruption thrive. A world of flawed men and women.

Play it if you want to:

  • Perform dark deeds in a grim world
  • Play anti-heroes who only succeed in spite of themselves

The Sorcerer

Gone are the ancient magi who mastered the arcane arts. All that is left is you. Your magic a pale shadow of their ancient masterworks.

Though your arts are unimpressive, you eke out a modest living doing what others cannot (or won't) do.

1. Choose a name

2. Choose a title

Sorcerer, Warlock, Witch, or Wizard

3. Stats

Your stats are Body -1, Face +0, Hand +0, Mind +2, Spirit +1

4. Choose a look

5. Choose a flaw

Choose a flaw and gain the corresponding moves.

Bumbling

Hedge

You are a laughing stock and most people don't even believe you can do magic. Your ham-fisted attempts to prove it only make them laugh harder. One day you'll show them.

  • Adjust your Face by -1

Wicked

When you succeed at an Aid or Hinder, or Parlay move on a roll of 10+ you can choose to fail that roll. If you do hold 1 and choose one:

  • You whisper a terrible secret to the character making them Disturbed.
  • You scratch or bite the character, making them hostile to you or weakening your bond.

You may spend hold to accidentally succeed at a failed move.
