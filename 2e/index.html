Three Meet

2nd Edition

A low-fantasy game for dark times. Three Meet is adapted from Pathfinder 2nd Edition, and offers simpler play and grubbier characters.

Character Creation

Rule

Print a character sheet, and follow this step-by-step guide to create your character:

  • You start with 0 in each of your Attributes

Attributes

Rule

Attributes represent your raw potential and basic abilities. The higher the score, the greater the your potential in that Attribute.

The three Attributes are:

Might

Attribute

Raw strength, stamina and combat prowess.

Added to Health, Stamina, and Melee Attacks and Stress rolls.

Cunning

Attribute

Wits, speed, agility, dexterousness, charm, perceptiveness and guile.

Added to Initiative, Defence and Ranged Attacks and Stress rolls.

Wisdom

Attribute

Intelligence, intuition, knowledge, spirituality and ingeniousness.

Added to Spell Attacks.

Basic Rules

This section details a number of general rules of the game. These rules are used in every mode of play.

Checks

Rule

When success isn’t certain attempt a Check:

  1. Roll a d20 and identify the modifiers that apply.
  2. Add the relevant Attribute, and Proficiency if it applies.
  3. Compare the result to the Difficulty.
  4. Determine the degree of success and the effect.
12
+ Attribute + Proficiency

For example, Roche is attempting to lift a carriage trapping an ally. The GM asks him make a Average (15) Mighty Athletics Check. Roche rolls an 8. His Might is +2, his Proficiency is +2, and he is proficient in Athletics so he gets a total of 12:

12
+ 2Attribute + 2Proficiency = 12Result

Difficulty

Whenever you attempt a Check, you compare your result against a Difficulty. When calling for a Check, select a Difficulty from the table below:

Description Difficulty
Easy 10
Average 15
Hard 20
Very Hard 30
Incredibly Hard 40

Proficiency

Proficiency measures a character’s aptitude at a specific task or quality. It is determined by your Class and Level.

If you are Trained in a Skill, Tool, Weapon, Armour or Spell, you add your Proficiency to the Check.
